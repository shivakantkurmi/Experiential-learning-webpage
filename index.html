Hyderabad: Tech & Heritage

Nov 3, 2025 - Nov 7, 2025

The trip to Hyderabad, from November 3rd to 7th, was a fascinating journey through a city of contrasts. We delved into the heart of India's tech boom, visiting futuristic IT parks and innovation hubs. Juxtaposed with this modernity, we also explored the city's deep historical roots and magnificent heritage, witnessing how technology and tradition coexist and thrive together.
